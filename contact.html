Frequently Asked Questions

What is the expected shipping time?
Shipping time ranges from 2-5 business days within Cairo, and 3-7 days for other governorates. The duration may increase during holidays and seasons.
Can I return or exchange the product?
Yes, you can return or exchange the product within 7 days of receiving it, provided it is in its original condition and with the invoice.
How do I know my size?
We provide a detailed size guide on our website. You can also contact customer service for a free consultation.
What payment methods are available?
We offer multiple payment methods: Cash on Delivery, Bank Transfer, and Credit/Debit Cards.
